What Makes a Broker Good for Scalping?

Scalping requires specific broker characteristics that differ significantly from swing or position trading:

  • Raw spreads under 0.5 pips — every pip fraction impacts P&L on 50+ daily trades
  • Execution under 50ms — slippage on fast entries/exits destroys edge
  • No scalping restrictions — some brokers limit trade duration or EA usage
  • Deep liquidity — order book depth prevents large orders from moving price
