## Overview

HFM (formerly HotForex) is a Cyprus-based broker that has grown from a regional player into a global operation serving 3.5 million+ registered clients. The 2022 rebrand from HotForex reflected the company's evolution beyond pure forex into multi-asset trading with 3,500+ instruments spanning forex, stocks, indices, commodities, ETFs, and bonds.

The broker's most distinctive features are its 1:2000 maximum leverage (the highest among regulated brokers), $0 minimum deposit, and HFcopy trading platform. These features target traders who want maximum flexibility — from absolute beginners starting with minimal capital to aggressive traders seeking high leverage.

HFM's Zero account offers competitive raw spreads from 0.0 pips with a $3/lot commission, while the Pro account provides tighter spread-only pricing from 0.5 pips. The Premium account with wider spreads (1.2 pips) serves as the entry-level option with no minimum deposit requirement.

Regulated by CySEC and FCA, HFM provides Tier-1 safety for EU and UK clients. The 4.2 Trustpilot score from 7,500+ reviews reflects strong user satisfaction. International clients should note that DFSA and FSCA entities provide less regulatory protection than the EU/UK entities.

## Account Intro

HFM offers three account types designed for different experience levels and trading styles. The Zero minimum on Premium and Zero accounts makes HFM one of the most accessible regulated brokers.

## Account Outro

Demo accounts are free with virtual funds across all platforms. Islamic swap-free accounts are available. Cent accounts are offered for micro-lot trading with minimal risk. The HFcopy platform operates alongside regular trading accounts.

## Regulation

HFM holds dual Tier-1 regulation from CySEC (183/12) and FCA (801701). CySEC provides EU regulatory standards with segregated client funds, negative balance protection, and ICF coverage up to €20,000. The FCA license covers UK clients with FSCS protection up to £85,000.

DFSA (Dubai) and FSCA (South Africa) licenses cover Middle Eastern and African clients respectively. These Tier-2 regulators provide less comprehensive protection. The 1:2000 leverage is available only under certain entities and is not permitted under EU/UK regulation.

## Costs

HFM's Zero account delivers EUR/USD from 0.0 pips with a $3/lot commission ($6 round turn). With average spreads of 0.10 pips, total cost is approximately $7.00 per lot — competitive with IC Markets and Pepperstone.

The Pro account offers spreads from 0.5 pips with no commission — a total cost of approximately $5.00 per lot on EUR/USD. The Premium account's 1.2 pips ($12.00/lot) is wider but serves beginners who prioritize simplicity over cost optimization.

HFM charges no deposit fees. A $5/month inactivity fee applies after 6 months of no trading. Free VPS hosting is available for traders maintaining minimum volume requirements. Autochartist is provided free to all funded accounts.

## Deposits

HFM accepts deposits from $0 minimum via cards, Skrill, Neteller, crypto, and bank transfer. All methods are free. Electronic deposits are processed instantly. Bank transfers take 2-5 business days.

In our testing, a $100 card deposit was credited within 1 minute. A $50 Skrill withdrawal was processed within 24 hours. HFM supports USD, EUR, and NGN as account base currencies with additional regional currencies available.

## Platforms

The HFM Platform is a newly launched proprietary web and mobile application with modern design, integrated charting, and streamlined order placement. As a 2024 launch, it's still developing features but provides a clean alternative to MetaTrader.

MetaTrader 4 and MT5 are available with full functionality including Expert Advisors, custom indicators, and the MQL marketplace. HFM provides free VPS hosting and Autochartist integration for funded accounts.

HFcopy is the copy trading platform where followers select strategy providers based on performance metrics and risk scores. The platform is well-designed with transparent provider statistics. No cTrader or TradingView integration is available.

## Mobile

The HFM App provides mobile trading, copy trading, and account management on iOS and Android. The app integrates HFcopy functionality, allowing users to browse, follow, and manage copied traders from their phone.

Standard mobile features include charting, watchlists, order management, and push notifications. The HFcopy integration makes the mobile experience more engaging than standard MT4/MT5 mobile apps for social trading users.

## Support

HFM offers 24/5 customer support via live chat, email, and phone. Multi-language support covers 27+ languages. Our testing found live chat wait times averaging 3-7 minutes with helpful, friendly agents — consistent with the high Trustpilot score.

The HFM Help Center provides FAQs, platform tutorials, and trading guides. The support quality is genuinely good and reflects the company's focus on client satisfaction that drives the strong Trustpilot rating.

## Education

HFM provides educational content covering forex basics, technical analysis, and trading strategies through articles, video tutorials, and webinars. The education offering is solid and covers beginner to intermediate content.

Market analysis is published daily with technical outlooks and economic commentary. The Autochartist integration provides automated pattern recognition as an educational supplement. Overall education quality is good but below the benchmark set by IG Academy.

## Trustpilot

HFM has a 4.2/5 Trustpilot rating from about 7,560 verified reviews. Positive reviews highlight customer service, copy trading, and competitive pricing. Negative reviews mention withdrawal processing times and the HotForex legacy reputation. The strong score reflects genuine improvement in client experience since the rebrand.

## Country

HFM accepts clients from 180+ countries. Entity assignment: CySEC (EU), FCA (UK), DFSA (Middle East), FSCA (Africa), or other entities. US clients are not accepted. Maximum leverage varies significantly by entity — 1:30 under EU/UK, up to 1:2000 under other entities.

## Verdict

HFM delivers a compelling package for accessible multi-asset trading: $0 minimum deposit, competitive Zero account pricing, and well-implemented copy trading — all backed by dual Tier-1 CySEC+FCA regulation. The 4.2 Trustpilot score reflects genuine client satisfaction.

The concerns are the extremely high 1:2000 leverage (risky for inexperienced traders), the still-developing proprietary platform, and weaker regulation for international clients. For copy trading with tight spreads, HFM is a strong choice. For pure execution quality, IC Markets or Pepperstone are better. For education, XM leads the tier.
