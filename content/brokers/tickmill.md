## Overview

Tickmill is a London-headquartered ECN/STP broker that has carved out a niche as the lowest-cost option for active forex traders. Founded in 2014, the broker has grown rapidly by offering what many consider the best commission rates in the industry — $4 round-turn on the Pro account, approximately 43% cheaper than IC Markets and Pepperstone.

The broker's value proposition is straightforward: genuine ECN execution with raw spreads from 0.0 pips at the lowest possible commission. This laser focus on cost efficiency has attracted scalpers, day traders, and algorithmic traders who execute high volumes and where every fraction of a pip matters.

Tickmill offers 600+ tradable instruments across forex (62 pairs), stock indices, commodities, bonds, and cryptocurrencies. While the instrument range is smaller than larger competitors, it covers all major markets. Trading is available on MT4 and MT5 with free Autochartist integration for pattern recognition.

Regulated by FCA (UK) and CySEC (Cyprus), Tickmill provides dual Tier-1 protection with London headquarters. Client funds are held at Barclays under FCA segregation requirements. The FSA Seychelles entity serves clients in regions not covered by FCA or CySEC with higher leverage options.

## Account Intro

Tickmill offers three account types with clear differentiation. The Classic account suits beginners with spread-only pricing, while the Pro and VIP accounts target active traders with raw spreads and the industry's lowest commissions.

## Account Outro

All accounts support hedging, scalping, EAs, and news trading without restrictions. Islamic accounts available. Demo accounts provide $50,000 virtual balance. The key differentiator is commission — Pro at $4/lot and VIP at $3/lot round-turn.

## Regulation

Tickmill's FCA registration (717270) provides UK clients with robust protections including FSCS coverage up to £85,000, mandatory fund segregation at Barclays, negative balance protection, and regular FCA auditing. The London headquarters reinforces Tickmill's commitment to regulatory transparency.

CySEC regulation (278/15) extends Tier-1 protections to EU/EEA clients with the Investor Compensation Fund covering up to €20,000. The FSA Seychelles entity offers higher leverage (up to 1:500) but with correspondingly weaker protections. We recommend the FCA entity for maximum safety.

## Costs

Tickmill's trading costs are its defining feature. The Pro account charges $2.00 per lot, per side ($4.00 round-turn) — the lowest commission among major ECN brokers. Combined with average EUR/USD spreads of 0.08 pips, the total cost per standard lot is approximately $4.80.

The VIP account (minimum $50,000 equity) reduces commissions to $1.50/side ($3.00 round-turn), bringing total costs to approximately $3.80 per lot — a remarkable value for high-volume traders. The Classic account averages 1.6 pips all-in on EUR/USD with no commission.

No deposit fees, no withdrawal fees (one free per day), and no inactivity fee. Tickmill's transparent fee structure is a significant competitive advantage for cost-conscious traders who execute more than 5-10 lots daily.

## Deposits

Tickmill supports standard deposit methods including bank transfer, cards, e-wallets, and cryptocurrency with a $100 minimum across all options. All deposits are processed free of charge, with most electronic methods credited instantly.

In our testing, a card deposit of $300 was credited within 1 minute. A Neteller withdrawal of $200 was processed within 2 hours. Bank transfers took 2-3 business days. Tickmill supports USD, EUR, and GBP as base currencies.

## Platforms

Tickmill offers MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5, available on desktop, web, and mobile. Both platforms connect to Tickmill's ECN infrastructure with fast execution (30ms average). Free Autochartist integration provides automated chart pattern recognition.

MT4 remains the primary platform with full EA support, 30+ indicators, and one-click trading. MT5 adds more timeframes, a strategy tester for multi-currency backtesting, and an economic calendar. Myfxbook AutoTrade is available for copy trading.

The absence of cTrader and TradingView is Tickmill's main platform limitation. Traders who rely on cTrader's advanced order types or TradingView's charting will need to look at Pepperstone, IC Markets, or FP Markets. Free VPS is available for qualifying traders.

## Mobile

Mobile trading is available through MT4 and MT5 apps on iOS and Android with full functionality. Charts, indicators, order management, and push notifications are all supported. Performance is consistent with the desktop MT4/MT5 experience.

The mobile apps provide adequate functionality for monitoring and managing positions on the go. However, the standard MetaTrader mobile interface lacks the polish of proprietary mobile apps offered by competitors like Exness or Capital.com.

## Support

Tickmill provides 24/5 customer support via live chat, email, and phone from offices in London, Tallinn, and Kuala Lumpur. In our testing, live chat responses averaged 3-5 minutes with knowledgeable agents who could discuss trading conditions in detail.

Email responses were received within 6 hours. Support is available in 10 languages. Tickmill also offers dedicated account managers for VIP clients. The overall support experience is professional and efficient, though not as immediately responsive as Pepperstone's sub-2-minute average.

## Education

Tickmill's educational content is limited. The broker provides basic forex education articles, a glossary, and occasional webinars. There are no structured video courses or interactive learning platforms like those offered by IG or XM.

Research tools include daily market analysis, an economic calendar, and free Autochartist for pattern recognition. The research offering is adequate for experienced traders but insufficient for beginners seeking comprehensive learning resources.

## Verdict

Tickmill earns our Lowest Commission award for 2026. For active traders who prioritize cost efficiency above all else, Tickmill's $4 round-turn commission on the Pro account delivers the best value in the industry. Combined with raw spreads and FCA regulation, it's a compelling choice for scalpers and high-frequency traders.
