## Overview

IG is the world's oldest and most regulated online trading broker, founded in 1974 by Stuart Wheeler in London as "Investors Gold Index" — one of the first companies to offer financial spread betting. Now publicly listed on the London Stock Exchange (LSE: IGG), IG has evolved into a global financial institution serving 313,000+ active clients across 200+ countries.

What makes IG unique is the combination of heritage, regulation, and scale. With four Tier-1 licenses (FCA, ASIC, NFA, MAS), IG is arguably the safest broker available to retail traders. The acquisition of tastytrade in 2022 further expanded its US presence, making IG one of only two brokers legally accepting American forex traders.

IG offers an industry-leading 17,000+ tradable instruments spanning forex (80+ pairs), share CFDs (12,000+), indices, commodities, bonds, options, and sector instruments. UK clients can also access spread betting and share dealing through ISA accounts. The breadth of markets is unmatched by any competitor.

The IG Trading Platform is consistently rated the best proprietary platform in the industry. Combined with ProRealTime for advanced charting, L2 Dealer for DMA execution, MT4 for algorithmic trading, and IG Academy for education, the platform ecosystem provides everything from beginner tutorials to institutional-grade tools.

## Account Intro

IG offers a straightforward account structure. The Standard account handles most traders' needs with spread-only pricing. The DMA (Forex Direct) account provides raw spreads and commission for professional traders.

## Account Outro

IG also offers share dealing accounts, ISAs (UK), and SIPP (UK) for long-term investors. Demo accounts provide £10,000 in virtual funds. Islamic accounts are available for swap-free trading. Corporate and professional accounts unlock higher leverage.

## Regulation

IG's regulatory profile is the strongest in the retail trading industry. Four Tier-1 licenses — FCA (195355), ASIC (515106), NFA (0509630), and MAS (CMS100917) — cover virtually every major market. The FCA license, held since the regulator's inception, provides UK clients with FSCS protection up to £85,000.

As a publicly listed company on the London Stock Exchange, IG is subject to additional financial disclosure requirements, quarterly earnings reports, and independent auditing. This level of transparency is unmatched by any privately held broker. IG's balance sheet and client money are scrutinized by both regulators and public market analysts.

## Costs

IG's standard account offers competitive market maker pricing with EUR/USD averaging 0.60 pips and no commission — a total cost of approximately $6.00 per standard lot. While wider than ECN brokers, this is among the best market maker pricing available.

The DMA (Forex Direct) account provides raw spreads from 0.0 pips with volume-based commissions starting at $3.00 per $100,000 traded. For high-volume traders, the DMA account can rival ECN broker pricing while adding the benefit of IG's premium platform and research.

IG charges no deposit or withdrawal fees. A $12/month inactivity fee applies after 2 consecutive years without trading — easily avoided by maintaining any trading activity. ProRealTime costs £30/month but is waived with 4+ trades per month.
