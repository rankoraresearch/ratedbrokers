## Overview

IC Markets is one of the world's largest ECN forex brokers by daily trading volume, processing over $1 trillion in monthly transactions. Founded in 2007 in Sydney, Australia, by Andrew Budzinski, the broker was created to bridge the gap between institutional-grade execution and retail trading. Today, it serves over 200,000 active clients across 200+ countries.

What fundamentally distinguishes IC Markets from many competitors is its genuine ECN (Electronic Communication Network) execution model. Unlike B-book brokers — often referred to as "kitchen" brokers in the industry — that take the opposite side of their clients' trades, IC Markets routes all orders directly to a pool of 50+ liquidity providers including major banks like JPMorgan, Goldman Sachs, and Deutsche Bank. This means the broker has no financial incentive for its clients to lose money, which is a critical distinction that our team at RatedBrokers prioritizes.

IC Markets offers access to 2,250+ tradable instruments spanning forex (61 pairs), indices (25), commodities (22), cryptocurrencies (23), stocks (2,100+), bonds, and futures — all available as CFDs. The broker supports four trading platforms: MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, cTrader, and TradingView, with servers hosted in Equinix NY4 (New York) and LD5 (London) data centers for optimal latency.

The broker is regulated by two Tier-1 financial authorities — ASIC (Australian Securities and Investments Commission) and CySEC (Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission) — with an additional offshore entity under FSA Seychelles. Client funds are fully segregated in trust accounts at AA-rated Australian banks including Westpac and National Australia Bank.

## Account Intro

IC Markets offers three live account types, all with a $200 minimum deposit. The key difference is in pricing: the Standard account wraps all costs into the spread, while the two Raw Spread accounts offer tighter spreads plus a per-lot commission.

## Account Outro

All account types provide access to the same instrument range and support hedging, scalping, and Expert Advisor (EA) trading without restrictions. IC Markets also offers Islamic (swap-free) accounts and demo accounts with virtual $100,000 balances for practice.

## Regulation

Regulation is the most heavily weighted factor in our scoring methodology (25%). IC Markets holds licenses from three regulatory bodies, two of which are classified as Tier-1 — the highest category of financial regulation.

Under ASIC and CySEC regulation, IC Markets is required to maintain full segregation of client funds, provide negative balance protection, submit to regular external audits, and maintain minimum capital adequacy requirements. The Seychelles entity operates under lighter regulation — our recommendation is to open your account under the ASIC or CySEC entity when possible for maximum protection.

## Costs

Trading costs are a make-or-break factor for active traders. IC Markets earned a 9.9/10 in this category — the highest of any broker we've reviewed — thanks to its ultra-tight raw spreads and transparent commission structure.

IC Markets sources pricing from 50+ liquidity providers, creating deep order books with genuine interbank pricing. The Standard account has no commission but wider spreads (average 0.82 pips EUR/USD). The Raw Spread account is where IC Markets truly shines — with average spreads of just 0.02 pips on EUR/USD plus a $3.50 per lot, per side commission, the total cost per standard lot round-turn is approximately $7.02.

There are no hidden fees: no deposit fees, no withdrawal fees (1 free per day), no inactivity fees, and no account maintenance fees. Overnight swap rates are standard and published transparently on the IC Markets website.

## Platforms

IC Markets supports four trading platforms, offering flexibility for different trading styles. All platforms connect to the same liquidity pool and server infrastructure hosted in Equinix NY4 and LD5 data centers.

MetaTrader 4 remains the most popular platform among IC Markets clients, offering over 30 built-in indicators, Expert Advisor support, and one-click trading. MetaTrader 5 adds a built-in economic calendar, more timeframes (21 vs 9), and an improved strategy tester for algo trading. cTrader is the platform of choice for scalpers, offering Level II pricing, advanced order types (iceberg, TWAP), and a native C# automated trading environment.

The TradingView integration, launched in 2023, allows traders to execute orders directly from TradingView's charting interface — combining what many consider the best charting software with IC Markets' execution. Free VPS hosting is available for clients with $1,500+ equity, hosted in Equinix facilities for sub-1ms latency to IC Markets' servers.

## Mobile

IC Markets offers mobile trading through the MT4, MT5, and cTrader mobile apps, available on both iOS and Android. All apps provide full trading functionality including order placement, chart analysis, account management, and push notifications for price alerts.

The cTrader mobile app is particularly well-designed, offering a modern interface, advanced charting with 65+ indicators, and support for all order types including stop-limit and trailing stop orders. The IC Social app (powered by Pelican Exchange) allows mobile copy trading — you can browse and copy strategies from top-performing traders directly from your phone.

## Support

IC Markets offers 24/7 customer support via live chat, email, and phone. In our testing, live chat wait times averaged 3-5 minutes during off-peak hours and 10-15 minutes during peak Asian/London session overlap. Email responses were received within 6 hours. Support agents were knowledgeable about trading conditions, platform issues, and account-related queries.

The broker provides support in 18 languages and has dedicated offices in Sydney, Limassol, and Seychelles. While the support quality is good, the peak-hour wait times are a notable weakness compared to brokers like IG or OANDA which offer near-instant connections.

## Education

IC Markets' educational offering is its weakest area. The broker provides basic educational materials including trading tutorials, webinars, and a glossary. However, these resources lack the depth and structure found at competitors like IG Academy, AvaTrade's SharpTrader, or XM's educational hub.

Research tools are better: IC Markets offers daily market analysis through Trading Central, an economic calendar, the "IC Your Trade" podcast produced with Bloomberg Media Studios, and an Autochartist plugin for MT4/MT5 that provides automated chart pattern recognition. For traders who value research over education, the offering is adequate.

## Trustpilot

IC Markets has an outstanding Trustpilot reputation with a 4.8/5 rating from over 38,000 verified reviews. ## Country

IC Markets accepts clients from 200+ countries worldwide. Notable exclusions include the United States, Canada, Israel, Iran, and North Korea. The entity you are assigned to depends on your country of residence: ASIC (Australia, NZ), CySEC (EU/EEA countries), or FSA Seychelles (rest of world). UK clients are onboarded through the Seychelles entity, which means weaker regulatory protection than FCA-regulated alternatives.

IC Markets is our top-rated broker for 2026 and earns our Editor's Choice award. The combination of true ECN execution, raw spreads from 0.0 pips, dual Tier-1 regulation, and four trading platforms makes it the best option for serious traders who prioritize execution quality and low costs above all else.
