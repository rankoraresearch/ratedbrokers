All forex/CFD accounts support hedging, scalping, and EA trading. Islamic accounts are available. Demo accounts mirror live conditions with $100,000 in virtual funds. The IRESS account provides Level 2 market depth and conditional order types not available on MT4/MT5.

## Regulation

FP Markets holds dual Tier-1 regulation from ASIC (Australia, license 286354) and CySEC (Cyprus, license 371/18). Both regulators impose strict requirements including segregated client accounts, negative balance protection, capital adequacy, and regular external auditing. The FSCA (South Africa) license provides additional coverage for African clients.

Client funds under the ASIC entity are held in segregated trust accounts at National Australia Bank. CySEC clients are covered by the Investor Compensation Fund (ICF) up to €20,000. FP Markets also maintains professional indemnity insurance, providing an additional layer of protection that many competitors do not offer.
