## Overview

RoboForex is a Belize-headquartered broker renowned for offering one of the widest instrument selections in the industry — over 12,000 tradeable assets across forex, stocks, indices, commodities, ETFs, and cryptocurrencies. Founded in 2009, the company operates through two entities: RoboForex Ltd under FSC Belize for international clients and RoboMarkets under CySEC for EU clients.

The broker provides four distinct platforms — MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, cTrader, and the proprietary R StocksTrader — catering to different trading styles from scalping to long-term stock investing. The ECN account offers raw spreads from 0.0 pips with $4/lot commission, while the Prime account reduces this to $2/lot for high-volume traders.

What truly sets RoboForex apart is R StocksTrader, which provides real stock DMA (not just CFDs) to 12,000+ shares on US and European exchanges. This makes RoboForex one of the few brokers bridging the gap between CFD trading and real stock investing within a single ecosystem.

The 4.6/5 Trustpilot rating from over 12,350 reviews is among the highest in the online brokerage industry, reflecting exceptional client satisfaction across execution quality, withdrawal processing, and customer support.

## Account Intro

RoboForex provides four account types across different platforms and trading styles. From commission-free Pro accounts to the ultra-low Prime ECN and the unique R StocksTrader for real stocks, there is genuine specialization.

## Account Outro

Demo accounts are available across all platforms with unlimited duration. Islamic swap-free accounts can be requested. CopyFX social trading operates alongside regular accounts. Cent accounts are available for micro-position testing.

## Regulation

RoboForex operates a dual-entity structure: RoboMarkets Ltd holds CySEC license 191/13 for EU clients, providing Tier-1 regulation with segregated funds, negative balance protection, and ICF coverage up to €20,000. This is the stronger regulatory entity.

RoboForex Ltd holds FSC Belize license 000138/437 for international clients. While Belize is a Tier-3 jurisdiction with less regulatory oversight, RoboForex voluntarily maintains segregated client funds and provides a compensation fund. The 16-year track record without major incidents and the high Trustpilot score partially mitigate the regulatory concern.

RoboForex ECN account offers raw spreads from 0.0 pips with a $4/lot commission ($8 round turn). With EUR/USD averaging 0.10 pips, total cost is approximately $9.00 per standard lot. The Prime account reduces commission to $2/lot ($4 round turn) — approximately $5.00 per lot total — but requires $10,000 minimum.

The Pro account provides commission-free trading with spreads from 1.3 pips — approximately $13.00 per standard lot on EUR/USD. R StocksTrader charges $0.015 per share (minimum $0.25/order) for real stock DMA.

No deposit fees are charged regardless of method. Withdrawal fees are generally free for e-wallets and crypto; bank transfers may incur small charges. No inactivity fee applies. The cost structure is competitive, particularly for Prime account holders.

## Platforms

MetaTrader 4 and MT5 provide the core trading experience with full EA support, custom indicators, and the MQL marketplace. Both platforms connect to ECN, Pro, and Prime account types with standard MetaTrader functionality.

cTrader adds depth-of-market (Level II) data, advanced order types, and cAlgo for algorithmic trading. The addition of cTrader gives RoboForex broader platform coverage than many competitors.

R StocksTrader is the proprietary platform offering real stock DMA, ETF trading, and multi-asset portfolios. The web-based interface provides charting, watchlists, and portfolio analytics. It is separate from the MT/cTrader forex accounts.

R StocksTrader has a web-based mobile interface for stock trading. The mobile experience is adequate but not as polished as dedicated stock trading apps from competitors like eToro or Capital.com.
