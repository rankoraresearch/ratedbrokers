## Overview

Axi (formerly AxiTrader) is a Sydney-based ECN broker with 18 years of operation under ASIC and FCA dual Tier-1 regulation — one of the longest track records among regulated ECN brokers. Founded in 2007, the company has built a reputation for reliable execution, transparent pricing, and useful trading tools like free Autochartist and PsyQuation analytics.

The broker offers $0 minimum deposit across all account types, including the Pro ECN account with raw spreads from 0.0 pips. Combined with free VPS hosting for active traders, Axi creates a particularly attractive proposition for algorithmic and EA-based trading strategies.

Axi's approach prioritizes execution quality and useful tools over sheer instrument breadth. While the 290+ instrument range is modest compared to competitors, each instrument benefits from deep liquidity sourced through ECN/STP infrastructure. The focus is depth over breadth.

The 4.4/5 Trustpilot score from 2,450+ reviews reflects consistent satisfaction with Axi's execution, support quality, and the practical utility of included tools. The ASIC + FCA regulatory backbone provides confidence that client funds and rights are well-protected.

## Account Intro

Axi offers three account types, all with $0 minimum deposit except Elite ($25,000). The Standard and Pro accounts differ primarily in pricing model (spread-inclusive vs raw + commission), while Elite offers premium rates for high-volume traders.

## Account Outro

Demo accounts are available with 30-day access (extendable on request). Islamic swap-free accounts available for qualifying clients. Axi Copy social trading operates alongside standard trading accounts. Corporate and institutional accounts available.

## Regulation

Axi holds ASIC (318232) and FCA (509746) dual Tier-1 regulation — both among the world's most respected financial regulators. Client funds are segregated in top-tier banks including National Australia Bank. ASIC clients benefit from AFCA (Australian Financial Complaints Authority) dispute resolution.

Additional regulation from FMA New Zealand (518226) and DFSA UAE (F003742) extends coverage to Asia-Pacific and Middle East. The multi-jurisdictional structure ensures broad geographic coverage while maintaining regulatory standards. The 18-year clean operating history with no significant regulatory actions reinforces trust.

## Costs

Axi's Pro account provides raw ECN spreads from 0.0 pips with a $7/lot commission ($14 round turn). With EUR/USD averaging 0.10 pips, total cost is approximately $8.00 per standard lot. This is competitive but above the cheapest ECN options (IC Markets $6.20, FXTM $5.50).

The Standard account offers commission-free trading with spreads from 1.0 pips — approximately $10.00 per lot on EUR/USD. The Elite account at $3.50/lot ($7 round turn) reduces total cost to approximately $4.50/lot — excellent value for qualifying high-volume traders.

Free VPS and Autochartist add tangible value. No deposit or withdrawal fees. No inactivity fee for 6 months; $10/month thereafter. The total cost of ownership considering free tools can offset the slightly higher commission.

## Deposits

Axi accepts deposits from $0 via cards, bank transfer, Skrill, Neteller, and POLi (Australia/NZ). All deposit methods are free. Electronic deposits process instantly; bank transfers take 1-3 business days. The $0 minimum applies to all methods.

In our testing, a card deposit was credited within seconds. Withdrawal processing took approximately 24 hours for e-wallets and 2-3 days for bank transfers. Axi supports AUD, USD, EUR, GBP, and NZD as base currencies.

## Platforms

MetaTrader 4 is Axi's primary platform with full EA support, custom indicators, and one-click trading. The MT4 offering is enhanced with free Autochartist (pattern recognition and trading signals) and PsyQuation (AI trading analytics) — tools that add genuine analytical value.

Axi Trading Platform is the newer proprietary web and mobile platform launched in 2023. It provides a modern interface with streamlined charting, watchlists, and order management. While still maturing, it offers a cleaner alternative to MT4 for non-algo traders.

The absence of MT5, cTrader, and TradingView is a notable gap. Traders requiring these platforms should consider Pepperstone (all four available) or BlackBull Markets (five platform choices). Axi's strength is in the MT4 + tools ecosystem rather than platform variety.

## Mobile

The MT4 mobile app provides standard mobile trading with charting, order management, and account monitoring. Axi Trading Platform has a responsive mobile version offering a modern alternative.

Mobile trading covers essential functions but doesn't distinguish itself from competitors. The real mobile value comes from PsyQuation analytics being accessible on mobile for performance tracking on the go.

## Support

Axi provides 24/5 customer support via live chat, email, and phone. Multi-language support in 14+ languages. Our testing found live chat response times averaging 2-4 minutes with knowledgeable agents who understand ECN execution and platform technical details.

The 18-year operating history has built a mature support infrastructure. Elite account holders receive dedicated account managers and priority support channels. The Help Center provides comprehensive self-service resources.

## Education

Axi provides educational content including trading courses, market analysis, strategy guides, and webinars. The education quality is good for intermediate topics but less comprehensive than specialist educators like IG Academy.

PsyQuation itself serves an educational function — by analyzing your trading behavior and highlighting mistakes, it provides personalized learning. Combined with Autochartist's pattern education, Axi's tools-based education is unique and practical.

## Verdict

Axi is a mature, well-regulated ECN broker that delivers reliability over flash — 18 years of ASIC+FCA operation, genuine ECN execution, and practical free tools (VPS, Autochartist, PsyQuation) create a solid foundation for serious traders. The $0 minimum deposit and clean pricing model make it accessible at all levels.

The limitations are the modest instrument range (290+), platform constraints (MT4 and new Axi Platform only), and Pro commission ($7/lot) that's above the cheapest ECN options. For maximum regulation with ECN execution, Axi competes directly with Pepperstone — which offers more platforms and instruments but without the free VPS and analytics tools.
