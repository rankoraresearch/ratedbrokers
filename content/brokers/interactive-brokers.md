## Overview

Interactive Brokers (IBKR) is not just a forex broker — it's a global financial institution providing access to 150+ exchanges and market centers across 200+ countries, all from a single unified account. Founded in 1978 by Thomas Peterffy, a pioneer of electronic trading, IBKR is listed on NASDAQ and manages $15T+ in client equity.

IBKR's forex offering is a subset of its massive multi-asset platform. Traders can access 100+ forex pairs with ECN/DMA execution, Smart Order Routing, and institutional-grade pricing from 0.1 pips + $2/lot commission. The same account provides access to stocks, options, futures, bonds, mutual funds, and crypto across global markets.

The Trader Workstation (TWS) is the most powerful retail trading platform available, offering advanced order types, algorithmic trading, portfolio analytics, risk management tools, and market scanners. However, this power comes at the cost of complexity — TWS has the steepest learning curve of any retail platform.

For beginners, IBKR offers two simplified alternatives: IBKR Lite with $0 commissions on US stocks, and IBKR GlobalTrader — a mobile app designed for a simpler trading experience. These products bridge the gap between IBKR's institutional heritage and retail accessibility.

## Account Intro

IBKR offers two main account types: IBKR Lite for casual investors with $0 US stock commissions, and IBKR Pro for active traders with best execution and tiered pricing. Both have no minimum deposit.

## Account Outro

IBKR also offers accounts for advisors, hedge funds, proprietary trading groups, and family offices. The IBKR Campus provides free educational content including courses, webinars, and trading simulations.

## Regulation

Interactive Brokers has the most comprehensive regulatory coverage of any retail broker. Five Tier-1 licenses — SEC/FINRA (US), FCA (UK), ASIC (Australia), MAS (Singapore), and IIROC (Canada) — provide worldwide coverage. The company is publicly listed on NASDAQ (IBKR) with a market cap exceeding $50 billion.

US accounts are protected by SIPC coverage up to $500,000 (including $250,000 cash). IBKR also provides excess insurance through Lloyd's of London for up to $30 million per account. Client assets are held in segregated accounts across multiple custodian banks. IBKR's balance sheet transparency is unmatched due to SEC reporting requirements.

## Costs

IBKR Pro offers some of the lowest forex trading costs available from a Tier-1 regulated broker. EUR/USD spreads average 0.1 pips with a $2 per lot per side commission — approximately $5.00 total per standard lot. High-volume traders qualify for further tiered discounts.

IBKR Lite users pay slightly wider spreads with no explicit commission on most products. $0 commission applies to US-listed stocks and ETFs. Non-US stocks and forex still carry commissions on Lite accounts.

No deposit or withdrawal fees for most methods. One free withdrawal per month; additional withdrawals are $10 each. No inactivity fees on any account type since 2021. Interest earned on idle cash at competitive rates (currently 4.3% USD).

## Deposits

IBKR supports bank transfers, wire transfers, and ACH (US). Notably, credit/debit card deposits are NOT supported — a common complaint from retail traders accustomed to instant card funding. Bank transfers typically take 1-4 business days.

One free withdrawal per calendar month. Additional withdrawals cost $10 each. IBKR pays competitive interest on uninvested cash balances — currently 4.3% USD, which partially compensates for the less convenient funding options.

## Platforms

Trader Workstation (TWS) is IBKR's flagship desktop platform — the most powerful trading software available to retail clients. Features include 100+ order types, algorithmic trading tools (ScaleTrader, Accumulate/Distribute), portfolio risk analytics, market scanners, and customizable workspaces. TWS can do essentially anything an institutional terminal can.

IBKR Mobile provides a full-featured trading experience on iOS and Android with real-time quotes, charting, options chains, and account management. Client Portal is a web-based platform for simpler account management and basic trading. IBKR GlobalTrader is a simplified mobile app designed for beginners.

TWS supports FIX API and third-party platform integration for quantitative and algorithmic trading. The platform also offers paper trading (simulated) accounts for strategy testing.

## Mobile

IBKR Mobile is a comprehensive trading app supporting the full range of products — stocks, options, futures, forex, and more. Features include advanced charting, real-time streaming data, options strategy builder, and portfolio analytics.

IBKR GlobalTrader is a separate, simplified mobile app designed specifically for beginners and casual investors. It focuses on stock and ETF investing with a streamlined interface, fractional shares, and educational content.

## Support

IBKR offers 24/6 customer support via phone, email, live chat, and a secure message system in Client Portal. Response times can be slow — our testing found live chat waits of 10-25 minutes. Phone support was faster but often directed to automated menus.

The IBKR Knowledge Base is comprehensive with detailed articles, video tutorials, and the IBKR Campus educational platform. Support quality improves significantly for higher-value accounts and institutional clients.

## Education

IBKR Campus is a comprehensive educational platform offering free courses on trading, investing, market analysis, and platform usage. Content covers everything from beginner stock investing to advanced options strategies and quantitative trading.

IBKR's Traders' Insight blog provides daily market commentary, research, and trade ideas. The company also hosts regular webinars with industry experts. For quantitative traders, IBKR provides extensive API documentation and development resources.
