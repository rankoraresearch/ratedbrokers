## Overview

CMC Markets is one of the pioneers of online trading, founded in 1989 by Peter Cruddas (now Lord Cruddas) in London. With 36 years of continuous operation and a listing on the London Stock Exchange (CMCX), CMC Markets has established itself as one of the most trusted and feature-rich brokers available to retail traders.

The standout feature is the Next Generation trading platform — a proprietary web-based platform that rivals IG's platform for the title of best proprietary trading interface. With 115+ technical indicators, pattern recognition scanning, client sentiment data, and integrated Reuters news, it provides professional-grade analysis tools at no additional cost.

CMC Markets offers 12,000+ instruments with a notable strength in forex — 330+ currency pairs represent the broadest forex selection of any broker. Share CFDs, indices, commodities, ETFs, and themed share baskets round out a comprehensive multi-asset offering suitable for diversified trading strategies.

Four Tier-1 regulatory licenses (FCA, ASIC, MAS, BaFin) and the LSE listing provide institutional-grade safety. The company serves 80,000+ active clients and consistently appears in industry award lists for platform quality, charting tools, and overall trading experience.

## Account Intro

CMC Markets keeps its account structure simple. CFD and spread betting accounts share the same pricing and platform access. The Alpha program provides volume-based rebates for active traders.

## Account Outro

Demo accounts are free with $10,000 virtual funds and full Next Generation platform access. Islamic swap-free accounts are available. Corporate accounts and professional status unlock higher leverage limits. No ECN or raw spread accounts are offered.

## Regulation

CMC Markets holds four Tier-1 licenses: FCA (173730), ASIC (238054), MAS (CMS100456), and BaFin (154814). The FCA license, held since 1996, is one of the longest-standing in the industry. UK clients benefit from FSCS protection up to £85,000 and negative balance protection.

As a publicly listed company on the London Stock Exchange, CMC Markets is subject to quarterly financial reporting, independent auditing, and stringent corporate governance requirements. This level of transparency provides accountability beyond what any private broker can offer.

## Costs

CMC Markets offers spread-only pricing with no commissions. EUR/USD averages 0.70 pips — competitive for a premium market maker and comparable to IG (0.60 pips). Total cost per standard lot is approximately $7.00.

The Alpha program provides cash rebates for active traders based on monthly notional volume. Qualifying traders receive spread rebates automatically credited to their account, effectively reducing trading costs by up to 15% for high-volume participants.

CMC charges no deposit fees, no withdrawal fees, and no inactivity fees — one of the cleanest fee structures among premium brokers. Overnight financing rates are standard. Currency conversion applies for non-base-currency instruments at competitive rates.

## Deposits

CMC Markets offers $0 minimum deposit with free funding via cards, PayPal, and bank transfer. Most electronic methods are credited instantly. Bank transfers take 1-3 business days. No deposit or withdrawal fees apply.

In our testing, a £300 card deposit was credited within 45 seconds. A £200 withdrawal to bank account was processed within 1 business day. CMC supports GBP, USD, EUR, AUD, CAD, NOK, NZD, PLN, SEK, and SGD as account base currencies.

## Platforms

The Next Generation platform is CMC's flagship offering — a web-based platform with 115+ technical indicators, 12 chart types, 70+ drawing tools, and a pattern recognition scanner that identifies chart formations in real-time across all instruments. Reuters news and client sentiment data are integrated directly into the trading interface.

The pattern recognition scanner and client sentiment tools are particular standouts. The scanner identifies formations like head-and-shoulders, double tops, and wedges across thousands of instruments simultaneously. Client sentiment shows the percentage of CMC traders long vs short on each instrument.

MetaTrader 4 is available as an alternative for traders who prefer EA-based trading. However, the MT4 offering is basic compared to the Next Generation platform. There is no MT5, cTrader, or TradingView integration — CMC focuses resources on its proprietary platform.

## Mobile

CMC's mobile app brings most of the Next Generation platform's features to iOS and Android. Charting includes 40+ indicators, drawing tools, and customizable watchlists. The app supports full order management including guaranteed stop-loss orders.

The mobile experience is smooth with quick data loading and responsive charting. Push notifications, price alerts, and economic calendar are integrated. While the mobile charting is less comprehensive than the web platform (40+ vs 115+ indicators), it remains one of the better mobile trading experiences available.

## Support

CMC Markets provides 24/5 customer support via phone, email, and live chat from offices in London, Sydney, Singapore, and other global locations. Our testing found live chat wait times averaging 5-12 minutes — occasionally slow during peak UK trading hours.

The support centre includes comprehensive FAQs, platform tutorials, and video guides. Dedicated account managers are available for high-value clients. Overall support quality is professional but can be inconsistent in response times during busy periods.

## Education

CMC Markets provides educational content through its Learn section, covering trading basics, platform tutorials, and market analysis. Content quality is good but the education offering is less structured than IG Academy. Weekly market outlook webinars are available.

The real educational value comes from the platform itself — pattern recognition scanning, client sentiment data, and integrated Reuters analysis provide real-time learning opportunities. The CMC Markets blog delivers daily market commentary and trading ideas.

## Trustpilot

CMC Markets has a 4.1/5 Trustpilot rating from about 7,830 verified reviews. Positive reviews praise the Next Generation platform, instrument range, and lack of inactivity fees. Negative reviews mention spread widening during volatility, mobile app limitations, and customer support response times. The rating is strong for a market maker broker.

## Country

CMC Markets accepts clients from 60+ countries. Entity assignment: FCA (UK/Europe), ASIC (Australia), MAS (Singapore), BaFin (Germany), or CMC International. US clients are not accepted. UK clients can access both CFD trading and spread betting. Leverage limits vary by jurisdiction.

## Verdict

CMC Markets earns our Best Platform award for 2026. The Next Generation platform is a masterpiece of retail trading technology — 115+ indicators, pattern recognition, client sentiment, and Reuters news create an analytical environment that rivals institutional terminals. Combined with 12,000+ instruments and four Tier-1 licenses, CMC delivers a premium trading experience.

The market maker model means spreads are wider than ECN brokers, and the lack of MT5, cTrader, or TradingView limits options for traders embedded in those ecosystems. For pure forex cost, IC Markets or Pepperstone are superior. But for traders who value platform quality, analysis tools, and the widest forex pair selection (330+), CMC Markets is an outstanding choice.
