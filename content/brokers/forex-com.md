## Regulation

FOREX.com's regulatory profile is among the strongest in the industry. As part of publicly traded StoneX Group, the broker is subject to SEC reporting requirements in addition to direct regulation by four Tier-1 authorities: NFA/CFTC (US), FCA (UK), IIROC (Canada), and ASIC (Australia).

US client funds are held in segregated accounts at major banks as required by NFA regulations. The CFTC's strict regulatory framework includes mandatory risk disclosure, leverage limits (1:50), and prohibition of hedging and FIFO enforcement — providing the highest level of client protection available.

No deposit or withdrawal fees for standard methods. No inactivity fee for accounts with open positions. Wire transfer withdrawals may incur bank fees. Active Trader discounts available for high-volume traders.
