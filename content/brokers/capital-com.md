## Account Intro

Capital.com offers three account tiers based on deposit level. All accounts are commission-free with costs built into spreads. Higher tiers receive tighter spreads and priority support.

## Account Outro

Demo accounts with $10,000 virtual funds are available. Islamic swap-free accounts can be requested. All account tiers access the same instruments and AI features — the difference is primarily in spread pricing and support priority.

## Regulation

Capital.com holds triple Tier-1 regulation: FCA (793714) in the United Kingdom, CySEC (319/17) in Cyprus, and ASIC (514907) in Australia. This is among the strongest regulatory profiles for any CFD broker, providing comprehensive protection across multiple jurisdictions.

Client funds are segregated in top-tier banks separate from company operating funds. Negative balance protection is guaranteed across all entities. EU clients receive ICF coverage up to €20,000, UK clients receive FSCS protection up to £85,000, and Australian clients benefit from ASIC's strict regulatory framework.

## Costs

Capital.com operates a commission-free model with costs built entirely into the spread. EUR/USD spreads average 0.8 pips on Standard accounts — approximately $8.00 per standard lot. Plus accounts reduce this to 0.5 pips ($5.00/lot) and Premier to 0.4 pips ($4.00/lot).

There are no deposit or withdrawal fees regardless of payment method. No inactivity fee is charged. Overnight financing (swap) rates apply to positions held past market close, which is standard for CFD brokers.

Compared to other commission-free brokers, Capital.com's Standard spreads are competitive with eToro (1.0 pips) and Plus500 (0.8 pips). The Plus and Premier tiers offer meaningfully tighter pricing for active traders willing to deposit more.

Capital.com accepts deposits from $20 via cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, bank transfer, and e-wallets including Skrill and Neteller. All methods are free with no Capital.com-imposed fees. Electronic deposits are instant; bank transfers take 1-3 business days.

## Platforms

Capital.com's flagship is its proprietary web and mobile platform featuring integrated AI technology. The platform provides smart newsfeeds tailored to your portfolio, sentiment indicators showing the percentage of traders long vs short, and pattern recognition that identifies chart formations automatically.

TradingView integration brings institutional-grade charting to Capital.com, allowing traders to execute directly from TradingView charts. This combination of AI insights from the proprietary platform and advanced charting from TradingView creates a powerful analytical environment.

MetaTrader 4 is available for traders who prefer algorithmic trading with Expert Advisors. However, MT5 is not offered, which limits some advanced features. The proprietary platform is clearly the primary focus of Capital.com's development efforts.

## Mobile

Investmate, the separate educational app, complements the trading app with structured courses and quizzes. Both apps share account integration, allowing seamless movement between learning and trading.

## Support

Plus and Premier account holders receive priority support with dedicated account managers. The multilingual support team covers 13+ languages. The Help Center provides comprehensive self-service resources including video tutorials and FAQ articles.

Beyond Investmate, Capital.com provides in-platform educational content including market analysis, trading guides, and webinars. The AI-powered insights themselves serve an educational function by explaining why certain patterns or sentiment shifts are significant.

## Verdict

Capital.com earns its place through genuine innovation — the AI-powered platform features, triple Tier-1 regulation, and commission-free model create an appealing package for both beginners and active traders. The $20 minimum deposit and Investmate education app make the entry barrier minimal, while Plus and Premier tiers provide meaningfully better pricing for larger accounts.

The limitations are the market-maker-only model (no ECN/DMA for those who want it), spread widening during volatility, and no MT5 support. For AI-enhanced trading with simple pricing, Capital.com is among the best. For raw ECN execution, look to IC Markets or Pepperstone instead.
