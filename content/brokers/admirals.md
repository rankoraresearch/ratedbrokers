## Overview

Admirals (formerly Admiral Markets) is an Estonian-born broker that has built one of the strongest reputations in the industry over 24 years. With three Tier-1 regulatory licenses and the highest Trustpilot score among major brokers (4.3/5), Admirals demonstrates that quality service and competitive pricing can coexist.

The broker distinguishes itself through its MetaTrader Supreme Edition — a significantly enhanced version of MT4/MT5 that adds professional tools like the mini terminal, correlation matrix, and market sentiment indicators. These additions transform the standard MetaTrader experience into something approaching institutional-grade capability.

Admirals offers 8,000+ instruments across three account types: Trade.MT5 for commission-free CFD trading, Zero.MT5 for raw spread execution, and Invest.MT5 for real stock and ETF ownership. This breadth makes Admirals a genuine multi-asset platform serving both traders and investors from a $25 minimum deposit.

Regulated by FCA, CySEC, and ASIC, Admirals provides strong safety across major jurisdictions. The 2021 rebrand from Admiral Markets reflected a broader strategic shift toward multi-asset investing beyond traditional forex, while maintaining the trading infrastructure that built its reputation.

## Account Intro

Admirals offers three distinct account types serving different needs. The Trade and Zero accounts target CFD traders with different pricing models, while Invest.MT5 provides real stock ownership for long-term investors.

## Account Outro

Demo accounts are free with virtual funds across all account types. Islamic swap-free accounts are available. Professional accounts unlock higher leverage. The $25 minimum on trading accounts and $1 minimum on Invest makes Admirals one of the most accessible triple-regulated brokers.

## Regulation

Admirals holds three Tier-1 licenses: FCA (595450), CySEC (201/13), and ASIC (410681). The home jurisdiction is Estonia under the EFSA (Estonian Financial Supervision Authority). Client funds are segregated under all entities with negative balance protection and investor compensation schemes.

The FCA entity provides FSCS protection up to £85,000 for UK clients. CySEC covers EU clients with ICF protection up to €20,000. ASIC provides Australian regulatory standards. The triple Tier-1 framework, combined with 24 years of clean operating history, positions Admirals among the safest mid-tier brokers.

## Costs

The Trade.MT5 account offers EUR/USD from 0.5 pips with no commission — a total cost of approximately $6.00 per standard lot. This is competitive with IG and CMC Markets while offering a lower minimum deposit.

The Zero.MT5 account provides raw spreads from 0.0 pips with a $3 per lot commission ($6 round turn). With average EUR/USD spreads of 0.10 pips, total cost is approximately $7.00 per lot — comparable to Pepperstone and IC Markets.

The Invest.MT5 account charges $0.02 per share (minimum $1 per order) for US stocks and 0.1% for European stocks. No custody fees apply. Admirals charges no deposit fees and offers one free withdrawal per month (€5 fee thereafter).

## Deposits

Admirals accepts deposits from $25 via cards, Skrill, Neteller, Klarna, and bank transfer. Most electronic methods are processed instantly with no fees. Bank transfers take 1-3 business days. One free withdrawal per month; subsequent withdrawals incur a €5 fee.

In our testing, a €100 card deposit was credited within 30 seconds. A €75 Skrill withdrawal was processed same day. Admirals supports EUR, USD, GBP, CHF, BGN, CZK, HRK, HUF, PLN, and RON as account base currencies.

## Platforms

MetaTrader 4 and MT5 are available in their Supreme Edition variants, which add the mini terminal (advanced order management), correlation matrix, market sentiment, session map, and global opinion widget. These tools are free for all funded accounts and significantly enhance the standard MetaTrader experience.

Premium Analytics is Admirals' research portal, combining Trading Central signals, Dow Jones newswire, and economic calendar into a single interface. The integration provides real-time trading ideas backed by professional analysis — a valuable addition to the MetaTrader toolkit.

SteamTrader is Admirals' newer proprietary web platform offering a modern interface for basic trading and portfolio management. While still developing, it provides a clean alternative to MetaTrader for simple order execution and account overview. No cTrader or TradingView integration is available.

## Mobile

Admirals' mobile experience relies on the standard MT4/MT5 mobile apps, which provide core trading functionality on iOS and Android. The Supreme Edition add-ons are not available on mobile, limiting the enhanced features to desktop and web.

The Admirals App provides account management, deposit/withdrawal functionality, and basic market overview. While functional, the mobile experience is less distinctive than the desktop MetaTrader Supreme Edition that defines the Admirals trading experience.

## Support

Admirals offers customer support via live chat, email, and phone during market hours. Multi-language support covers 20+ languages. Our testing found live chat wait times averaging 5-12 minutes with helpful responses for account and platform questions.

Support quality is generally good during European trading hours but can be slower during Asian or US sessions. The Help Center provides comprehensive articles, video tutorials for Supreme Edition tools, and platform setup guides.

## Education

Admirals provides solid educational content through its Zero to Hero trading course — a structured program covering forex basics, technical analysis, and trading psychology. The course is free and well-produced, though less comprehensive than IG Academy.

Regular webinars, daily market analysis, and the Premium Analytics suite serve as ongoing educational resources. The educational approach is practical, focusing on actionable knowledge rather than theoretical depth.

## Trustpilot

Admirals has a 4.3/5 Trustpilot rating from about 6,780 verified reviews — the highest score among major brokers. Positive reviews consistently praise customer service quality, platform reliability, and the educational content. Negative reviews occasionally mention withdrawal fees and execution speed. The exceptionally high rating reflects genuine attention to client experience.

## Country

Admirals accepts clients from 130+ countries. Entity assignment: FCA (UK), CySEC (EU), ASIC (Australia), or EFSA (Estonia/international). US clients are not accepted. Instrument availability and leverage limits vary by entity and jurisdiction. The Invest.MT5 account is available under EU and UK entities.

## Verdict

Admirals is a well-rounded broker that excels in user satisfaction — evidenced by its industry-leading Trustpilot score. The MetaTrader Supreme Edition transforms the familiar MT4/MT5 experience with professional tools, while the Invest.MT5 account adds genuine stock investing capability. Triple Tier-1 regulation and a $25 minimum make it accessible and safe.

The limitations are moderate execution speed compared to top ECN brokers, a still-developing proprietary platform, and the entity-dependent instrument availability. For traders who value MetaTrader enhancements and a broker with proven customer service, Admirals is an excellent choice. For raw execution speed, IC Markets or Pepperstone remain superior.
