## Overview

FxPro is a Cyprus-based broker that has earned its reputation through an unusually broad platform offering — four distinct trading environments under one roof. Founded in 2006, FxPro serves 2 million+ registered clients with access to MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, cTrader, and the proprietary FxPro Edge platform.

The cTrader implementation is particularly noteworthy. FxPro was one of the earliest brokers to adopt cTrader, and the NDD (No Dealing Desk) execution on this platform provides genuine interbank pricing without dealer intervention. This makes FxPro's cTrader account a legitimate alternative to ECN-focused brokers.

FxPro offers 2,100+ instruments across forex (70+ pairs), indices, commodities, shares, futures, and cryptocurrencies. While the instrument count is modest compared to IG or Saxo Bank, it covers the most actively traded markets comprehensively. The Raw+ account brings raw spread pricing to MetaTrader users.

Regulated by FCA and CySEC, FxPro has maintained a clean 18-year operating record. The company has received numerous industry awards for platform quality and execution. International clients should note they may be onboarded under the FSCA (South Africa) or SCB (Bahamas) entities with less regulatory protection.

## Account Intro

FxPro offers three account types, each mapped to a different execution model. The Standard account uses market maker pricing, while Raw+ and cTrader provide raw interbank spreads with commission.

## Account Outro

Demo accounts are available on all four platforms with $500,000 virtual funds. Islamic swap-free accounts can be requested. There is no VIP or tiered pricing structure — all traders access the same conditions within each account type.

## Regulation

FxPro holds dual Tier-1 regulation from FCA (509956) and CySEC (078/07). The FCA license covers UK clients with FSCS protection up to £85,000, while CySEC provides ICF coverage up to €20,000 for European clients. Both entities require segregated client funds and negative balance protection.

International clients outside the UK and EU may be assigned to the FSCA (South Africa) or SCB (Bahamas) entities. The FSCA provides reasonable protection but is Tier-2, while the SCB offers minimal regulatory oversight. Traders should confirm their entity assignment before depositing.

## Costs

FxPro's Standard account offers EUR/USD from 0.6 pips with no commission — a total cost of approximately $6.00 per standard lot. This is competitive with IG and CMC Markets, though ECN alternatives offer lower total costs.

The Raw+ account provides raw spreads from 0.0 pips with a $3.50 per lot commission ($7 round turn). The cTrader account charges $4.50 per $100,000 commission on raw spreads. Both raw accounts are competitive but slightly more expensive than IC Markets ($6/round) or Pepperstone ($7/round).

FxPro charges no deposit fees, no withdrawal fees, and no inactivity fees. Overnight swap rates are standard. The absence of hidden fees makes cost calculations transparent and straightforward.

## Platforms

MetaTrader 4 and MT5 are available with full functionality including Expert Advisors, custom indicators, and the MQL marketplace. FxPro provides free VPS hosting for qualifying traders maintaining minimum monthly trading volumes.

cTrader is FxPro's premium execution platform with Level 2 pricing, NDD execution, cAlgo for algorithmic trading, and advanced order types. The implementation is mature and well-maintained, with fast order routing and transparent pricing.

FxPro Edge is the proprietary web-based platform offering clean design, integrated charting, and simplified order management. While less feature-rich than xStation 5 or Next Generation, it provides a solid alternative for traders who prefer a lightweight interface. Trading Central and AutoChartist are integrated across all platforms.

## Mobile

FxPro's mobile app is available on iOS and Android with trading access across all platform types. The app includes charting, watchlists, order management, and social trading features. Copy trading functionality allows following successful traders.

The mobile experience is clean and functional with responsive data loading and smooth order execution. Push notifications, price alerts, and economic calendar are integrated. The social trading features, while basic compared to eToro, add value for mobile users.

## Support

FxPro offers 24/5 customer support via live chat, email, and phone. Multi-language support is available in 20+ languages. Our testing found live chat wait times averaging 4-10 minutes with generally helpful responses.

The FxPro Help Centre provides searchable articles covering platform setup, account management, and trading conditions. Video tutorials for each of the four platforms help new clients get started. Priority support is available for high-volume traders.

FxPro provides educational content through its Learn section, covering trading fundamentals, platform tutorials, and risk management. The content is solid but not as comprehensive as IG Academy or XM's structured courses.

Trading Central provides expert analysis and signals, while AutoChartist offers automated pattern recognition across instruments and timeframes. The daily FxPro Blog delivers market commentary and trading ideas. These tools serve as both research and educational resources.

FxPro accepts clients from 170+ countries. Entity assignment: FCA (UK), CySEC (EU), FSCA (South Africa), or SCB (international). US clients are not accepted. Platform availability varies by entity — cTrader may not be available under all entities. Leverage limits follow ESMA rules for EU clients.

## Verdict

FxPro is the go-to broker for traders who want genuine platform choice. The ability to trade on MT4, MT5, cTrader, and FxPro Edge from a single account offers flexibility that few competitors can match. The cTrader NDD execution is particularly strong, providing real interbank pricing.

For pure ECN execution cost, IC Markets or Pepperstone offer better value. But for traders who value platform variety and the flexibility to switch between execution models, FxPro delivers a unique proposition.
