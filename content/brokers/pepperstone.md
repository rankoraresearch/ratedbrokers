## Overview

Pepperstone is one of Australia's largest forex and CFD brokers, now operating globally across 170+ countries with a strong reputation for competitive pricing and exceptional customer service. Founded in 2010 in Melbourne by Owen Kerr and Joe Davenport, the broker was built on the principle that retail traders deserve the same quality execution and pricing previously reserved for institutional clients.

What sets Pepperstone apart is its combination of a $0 minimum deposit with genuine ECN execution through the Razor account. This makes professional-grade trading accessible to traders of all experience levels — from beginners opening their first account to veteran scalpers trading hundreds of lots daily. The broker processes over $12 billion in daily trading volume, placing it among the world's top 10 forex brokers by volume.

Pepperstone offers access to 1,200+ tradable instruments across forex (60+ pairs), indices, commodities, cryptocurrencies, ETFs, and shares as CFDs. The broker supports four leading platforms — MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, cTrader, and TradingView — with free Smart Trader Tools providing 28 additional MT4/MT5 add-ons including correlation traders, sentiment indicators, and alarm managers.

The broker's regulatory profile is exceptional: three Tier-1 licenses from FCA (UK), ASIC (Australia), and CySEC (Cyprus), plus a DFSA license for Middle Eastern clients. Client funds are held in segregated accounts at Barclays (UK entity) and National Australia Bank (ASIC entity), with negative balance protection provided across all regulated entities.

## Account Intro

Pepperstone offers two main account types — Standard and Razor — both available with a $0 minimum deposit. The Standard account includes all costs in the spread, while the Razor account offers raw institutional spreads plus a commission.

## Account Outro

Both account types support hedging, scalping, EAs, and news trading without restrictions. Islamic (swap-free) accounts are available on request. Demo accounts come with $50,000 in virtual funds and mirror live trading conditions.

## Regulation

Pepperstone's triple Tier-1 regulation is its strongest differentiator. The FCA (UK), ASIC (Australia), and CySEC (Cyprus) licenses represent the highest standard of financial oversight available to retail traders, with strict requirements for capital adequacy, fund segregation, and regular auditing.

Under FCA regulation, UK clients benefit from FSCS protection up to £85,000 per person. ASIC-regulated Australian clients have funds segregated at NAB. CySEC clients are covered by the ICF up to €20,000. The DFSA entity provides access for UAE and Middle Eastern traders with a Tier-2 regulatory framework.

## Costs

Pepperstone earns a strong 9.6/10 for trading costs. The Razor account delivers raw spreads from 0.0 pips with a commission of $3.50 per lot, per side — matching the industry standard set by IC Markets and providing genuine institutional pricing to retail traders.

The Standard account wraps all costs into spreads averaging 1.0-1.3 pips on EUR/USD, suitable for beginners who prefer simple pricing. On the Razor account, the total cost per standard lot round-turn on EUR/USD averages approximately $8.00 (spread cost + $7 commission), which is highly competitive.

Pepperstone charges no deposit fees, no withdrawal fees, and removed its inactivity fee globally in 2021. Overnight swap rates are competitive, and the Active Trader program offers rebates of up to $3 per lot for clients trading 100+ lots per month.
