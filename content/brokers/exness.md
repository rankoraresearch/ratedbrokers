## Account Intro

Exness offers four account types ranging from the $1 minimum Standard account to the professional Raw Spread account. The hybrid model provides both spread-only and raw spread + commission options.

## Account Outro

All accounts support hedging, scalping, and EA trading. Islamic swap-free accounts are available. The Standard Cent account supports cent-lot trading for ultra-small position sizes. Demo accounts are available with unlimited virtual balance.

## Regulation

Exness holds two Tier-1 licenses: CySEC (178/12) for European clients and FCA (730729) for UK clients. Both regulators require client fund segregation, negative balance protection, and capital adequacy. Exness uniquely publishes quarterly financial reports audited by Deloitte, providing transparency rare among retail brokers.

The FSA Seychelles entity (SD025) serves clients outside the EU/UK with fewer restrictions but also less protection. This entity offers unlimited leverage and wider instrument access. We recommend choosing the CySEC or FCA entity when available for maximum client protection.
