## Overview

Eightcap is an Australian-headquartered ECN broker that has rapidly grown since its founding in 2009. Now regulated by three Tier-1 authorities (ASIC, FCA, CySEC), Eightcap has carved out a niche as a cost-effective ECN broker with an unusually strong platform offering that includes TradingView integration.

What differentiates Eightcap from other ECN brokers is the platform flexibility. While most ECN brokers are limited to MetaTrader, Eightcap offers MT4, MT5, AND TradingView as directly connected platforms — giving traders the best charting tools in the industry alongside raw ECN execution.

Eightcap's Raw account offers EUR/USD from 0.0 pips (averaging 0.06 pips) with $3.50 per lot per side commission — total costs of approximately $7.60 per standard lot. This is competitive with industry leaders like IC Markets and Pepperstone.

The broker has also positioned itself as a leader in cryptocurrency CFDs, offering 250+ crypto pairs — one of the largest selections available from any regulated broker. Combined with Capitalise.ai for code-free automated trading, Eightcap provides a modern, innovative trading experience.

## Account Intro

Eightcap offers two simple account types: Standard (spread-only) and Raw (raw spreads + commission). Both require only $100 minimum deposit and provide access to the same instruments and platforms.

## Account Outro

Demo accounts available with $100,000 virtual funds. Islamic swap-free accounts available on request. No VIP or premium account tiers — the same execution quality for all clients.

## Regulation

Eightcap holds three Tier-1 regulatory licenses: ASIC in Australia (391441), FCA in the United Kingdom (921296), and CySEC in Cyprus (246/14). This regulatory breadth is strong for an ECN broker of Eightcap's size.

Client funds are held in segregated accounts at National Australia Bank (NAB) for Australian clients and top-tier European banks for FCA/CySEC clients. While Eightcap is not publicly listed, its clean regulatory record since 2009 and multi-jurisdictional licensing demonstrate commitment to compliance.

## Costs

Eightcap's Raw account is designed for cost-conscious traders. EUR/USD averages 0.06 pips with $3.50/lot per side commission, bringing total costs to approximately $7.60 per standard lot. This is slightly above IC Markets ($7.00) but competitive with Pepperstone ($7.20).

The Standard account offers EUR/USD from 1.0 pips with no commission — straightforward pricing for beginners. No inactivity fees, no deposit/withdrawal fees for standard methods.

Swap rates are competitive. Crypto CFD funding costs are transparent. Overall, Eightcap's pricing is in the top tier for ECN brokers.

## Deposits

Eightcap offers free deposits via card (instant), e-wallets (Skrill, Neteller — instant), bank transfer (1-3 days), and cryptocurrency (BTC, USDT — 10-30 minutes). $100 minimum deposit for all methods.

Withdrawals are processed within 1-2 business days. One free withdrawal per month; additional withdrawals may incur a small fee. The crypto deposit option is a notable convenience for crypto-native traders.

## Platforms

Eightcap's platform suite is its strongest feature. MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, and TradingView are all available as directly connected platforms. This gives traders unmatched flexibility — use MT4 for EAs, MT5 for multi-asset, or TradingView for the best charting experience.

Capitalise.ai is integrated for code-free automated trading. Write strategies in plain English and the AI converts them into executable rules. This makes algorithmic trading accessible to traders without programming skills.

The TradingView integration provides 100+ indicators, drawing tools, multi-chart layouts, and community ideas directly connected to Eightcap's ECN execution — the best of both worlds.

## Mobile

Eightcap offers the standard MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5 mobile apps for iOS and Android. These provide full trading functionality including charting, alerts, and order management.

TradingView's mobile app can also be used for charting and analysis, though direct trading from TradingView mobile requires the web interface. The mobile trading experience is functional but not differentiated from other MT4/MT5 brokers.

## Support

Eightcap provides 24/5 customer support via live chat, email, and phone. Our testing found live chat response times of 3-10 minutes during market hours, with generally knowledgeable agents.

The Help Centre provides articles on account management, platform setup, and trading basics. Content is adequate but not as comprehensive as larger brokers. Support quality can be inconsistent during off-peak hours.

## Education

Eightcap offers basic educational content including trading guides, video tutorials, and a blog with market analysis. The Eightcap Labs section provides platform tutorials and trading tips.

Educational depth is limited compared to IG Academy or IBKR Campus. For in-depth learning, traders should supplement with external resources. The Capitalise.ai integration provides educational value through its strategy building tutorials.
