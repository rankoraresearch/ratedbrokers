## Overview

FXTM (ForexTime) is a Cyprus-headquartered broker that has carved out a distinctive position through the lowest ECN commission in the industry — just $2 per lot ($4 round turn) on its Advantage account. Founded in 2011 by Andrey Dashin, the company has grown to serve 2 million+ registered clients with particular strength in emerging markets across Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.

The broker's Advantage account delivers genuine ECN execution with raw spreads from 0.0 pips. Combined with the $2/lot commission, total trading costs of approximately $5.50 per lot on EUR/USD undercut most major ECN competitors. The Advantage Plus account offers an alternative with slightly wider spreads (0.5 pips) but zero commission.

FXTM Invest is the broker's copy trading platform, enabling investors to allocate funds to strategy managers and automatically replicate their trades. The platform provides transparent manager statistics including return, risk metrics, and drawdown history — creating a genuine social investing layer alongside traditional trading.

Regulated by CySEC and FCA, FXTM provides Tier-1 safety for EU and UK clients. The Trustpilot score of 4.3 from nearly 6,000 reviews reflects strong user satisfaction, particularly around customer service, education, and the copy trading experience. The $10 Micro account minimum makes the broker accessible to absolute beginners.

## Account Intro

FXTM offers three account types designed for different trading stages. The Micro account introduces beginners, the Advantage provides the lowest ECN commission, and the Advantage Plus delivers tight spreads without commission.

## Account Outro

Demo accounts are free across all platforms. Islamic swap-free accounts are available. No VIP tiers based on deposit — the three accounts serve distinct trading needs rather than rewarding higher balances. FXTM Invest operates alongside regular trading accounts.

## Regulation

FXTM holds dual Tier-1 regulation from CySEC (185/12) and FCA (600475). CySEC provides EU regulatory standards with segregated client funds and ICF protection up to €20,000. The FCA license covers UK clients with FSCS protection up to £85,000 and negative balance protection.

The FSCA (South Africa) license covers African clients — an important market for FXTM. Non-EU/UK clients may be assigned to offshore entities with higher leverage but less regulatory protection. The 14-year operating history without significant regulatory incidents supports trust.

## Costs

FXTM's Advantage account delivers the industry's lowest ECN commission at $2/lot ($4 round turn). With average EUR/USD spreads of 0.15 pips, total cost is approximately $5.50 per standard lot — cheaper than IC Markets ($6.20), Pepperstone ($7.00), or FP Markets ($7.00).

The Advantage Plus account offers spreads from 0.5 pips with no commission — approximately $5.00 per lot on EUR/USD. The Micro account at 1.5 pips ($15.00/lot) is designed for learning with micro lots rather than cost-efficient execution.

FXTM charges no deposit fees. Withdrawal fees vary by method (free for most e-wallets, small fees for bank transfers). No inactivity fees apply. The clean cost structure reinforces the value proposition for cost-conscious traders.

MetaTrader 4 and MT5 are FXTM's primary platforms with full EA support, custom indicators, and the MQL marketplace. The MT offering is standard — no enhanced add-ons or Supreme Edition-style upgrades.

FXTM Trader is a proprietary mobile-only application launched in 2023, providing a modern interface for on-the-go trading. It's designed for convenience rather than advanced analysis and integrates with FXTM Invest for copy trading management.

No cTrader, TradingView, or web-based proprietary platform is available. The platform selection covers core needs through MetaTrader but lacks the innovation seen in competitors like ThinkMarkets (ThinkTrader) or Vantage (ProTrader).
