## Overview

XTB is a publicly traded Polish broker that has grown from a local Warsaw operation into one of Europe's most recognized trading platforms. Founded in 2002 and listed on the Warsaw Stock Exchange (WSE: XTB), the company combines competitive forex pricing with zero-commission real stock trading — positioning itself as a genuine multi-asset alternative to both traditional forex brokers and stock platforms.

The xStation 5 platform is XTB's crown jewel. Unlike many brokers that rely on third-party platforms, XTB has invested heavily in proprietary technology. The result is a modern, feature-rich platform with a built-in stock screener, market heatmap, trader calculator, and performance analytics that rivals the best in the industry.

XTB offers 5,800+ instruments across forex, stocks, indices, commodities, ETFs, and cryptocurrencies. The zero-commission real stock and ETF trading (up to €100K monthly turnover) has attracted a growing base of retail investors alongside the traditional forex trading audience.

With three Tier-1 licenses (FCA, KNF, CySEC) and a public listing on the Warsaw Stock Exchange, XTB provides strong regulatory safety. The company has over 1 million client accounts and is the largest listed broker in Central and Eastern Europe by market capitalization.

## Account Intro

XTB simplifies account structure with a single standard account for all retail traders. Professional status is available for qualified clients seeking higher leverage and tighter spreads.

## Account Outro

Demo accounts are free with €100,000 in virtual funds valid for 30 days (unlimited for funded clients). Islamic swap-free accounts are available. No separate ECN, raw spread, or tiered account options exist — XTB targets simplicity.

## Regulation

XTB holds three Tier-1 regulatory licenses: FCA (522157), KNF (Polish Financial Supervision Authority), and CySEC (169/12). The KNF is one of Europe's strictest regulators with banking-level oversight standards. Client funds are segregated across all entities with negative balance protection.

As a company listed on the Warsaw Stock Exchange, XTB publishes quarterly financial results, annual reports, and is subject to independent auditing and corporate governance standards. The company's market capitalization exceeds €2 billion, reflecting institutional confidence in its business model.

## Costs

XTB's standard account offers EUR/USD from 0.5 pips with no commission — a total cost of approximately $5.00 per standard lot. This is tighter than most market makers including IG (0.60 pips) and CMC Markets (0.70 pips), making XTB one of the most cost-effective commission-free brokers.

Zero-commission real stock and ETF trading applies to the first €100,000 in monthly turnover. Above this threshold, a 0.2% commission applies. For most retail investors, the €100K limit covers their entire activity, making stock trading effectively free.

XTB charges no deposit or withdrawal fees and no account maintenance fees. The €10/month inactivity fee applies only after 12 months of no trading activity. Currency conversion fees apply for non-base-currency instruments at competitive rates.

## Deposits

XTB offers $0 minimum deposit with free funding via cards, PayPal, Skrill, and bank transfer. Polish traders can use Przelewy24 for instant domestic transfers. Most electronic methods are credited instantly.

In our testing, a €200 card deposit was credited within 30 seconds. A €150 bank withdrawal was processed within 1 business day. XTB supports EUR, USD, GBP, PLN, HUF, and CZK as account base currencies.

## Platforms

xStation 5 is XTB's proprietary web-based platform featuring clean design, fast execution, and integrated analytics. The platform includes 34+ technical indicators, multiple chart types, and drawing tools alongside a real-time stock screener that filters thousands of instruments by criteria.

The market heatmap provides a visual overview of market performance across sectors and asset classes. The trader calculator instantly computes margin requirements, pip values, and potential costs for any position. Performance analytics track your trading statistics with detailed breakdowns.

XTB does not offer MT4, MT5, cTrader, or TradingView. This is a deliberate choice to focus resources on xStation 5 development. While this limits options for EA traders, the proprietary platform is genuinely excellent and regularly wins industry awards.

## Mobile

The xStation mobile app provides a polished trading experience on iOS and Android. Full charting with indicators, one-tap trading, watchlists, and push notifications are all included. The app mirrors the web platform's clean design philosophy.

Real-time market data, economic calendar, and account management are seamlessly integrated. The mobile app also provides access to zero-commission stock trading. Performance is consistently smooth with quick order execution and responsive charting.

## Support

XTB offers 24/5 customer support via phone, email, and live chat from offices in Warsaw, London, and other European locations. Our testing found live chat wait times averaging 3-8 minutes with knowledgeable agents, particularly for platform-related questions.

Support is available in 13+ languages reflecting XTB's strong European presence. The Help Center provides platform tutorials, trading guides, and FAQ articles. Dedicated account managers are available for high-value clients.

## Education

XTB provides a comprehensive Trading Academy with video courses covering forex basics, technical analysis, fundamental analysis, and advanced strategies. Content is available in multiple languages and structured from beginner to advanced levels.

Daily market analysis, video commentary, and economic calendar coverage complement the educational content. XTB's research team produces regular reports and trading ideas. The overall education quality is good, though less comprehensive than IG Academy.

## Trustpilot

XTB has a 3.8/5 Trustpilot rating from about 5,640 verified reviews. Positive reviews highlight xStation 5 platform quality, zero-commission stocks, and competitive spreads. Negative reviews mention the inactivity fee, limited instrument range compared to larger brokers, and occasional withdrawal processing delays. The rating is reasonable for a market maker broker.

## Country

XTB accepts clients from 190+ countries. Entity assignment: FCA (UK), KNF (Poland/EU), CySEC (EU), or DFSA (Middle East). US clients are not accepted. Zero-commission stock trading availability varies by region. Leverage limits follow ESMA rules for EU clients (max 1:30).

## Verdict

XTB is a compelling choice for European traders seeking a modern multi-asset platform with competitive pricing. The xStation 5 platform, zero-commission stock trading, and 0.50 pip EUR/USD spread create a well-rounded package that competes effectively with larger rivals like IG and CMC Markets.

The limitations are clear: no MetaTrader, fewer instruments than premium competitors, and a market maker model. Traders needing ECN execution should look at IC Markets or Pepperstone. But for traders who value an excellent proprietary platform, simple pricing, and the convenience of stocks and forex on one account, XTB delivers outstanding value.
