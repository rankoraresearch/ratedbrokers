## Overview

City Index is one of the UK's oldest financial trading firms, founded in 1983 as a pioneer of financial spread betting. Now operating under StoneX Financial Ltd — a subsidiary of NASDAQ-listed StoneX Group Inc. (SNEX) — City Index combines four decades of UK trading heritage with the financial backing of a Fortune 500 company generating over $60 billion in annual revenue.

The broker offers 13,500+ tradable instruments spanning 80+ forex pairs, 6,300+ shares, 28+ indices, 25+ commodities, and bonds. UK clients can choose between tax-free spread betting (8,500+ instruments) and traditional CFDs (13,500+ instruments), all under FCA regulation with FSCS £85,000 protection.

City Index provides four trading platforms: a proprietary Web Trader, MetaTrader 4, an award-winning mobile app (Best Trading App 2024), and a TradingView integration that allows direct order execution from TradingView's industry-leading charts. The TradingView integration is a standout feature that positions City Index alongside only Pepperstone and a handful of other brokers offering this capability.

With FCA, ASIC, CySEC, and MAS licences across four jurisdictions, City Index holds a strong multi-regulatory profile. The StoneX parentage provides institutional-grade financial stability that few standalone brokers can match. For UK traders seeking a well-established spread betting provider with modern platform choices, City Index is a compelling option.

## Account Intro

City Index offers flexible account options with spread betting and CFD trading available through the same account. MT4 users can open a dedicated MT4 account. Professional status unlocks higher leverage.

## Account Outro

All accounts come with zero minimum deposit (though cards require £100 minimum transaction). Demo accounts are available with virtual funds for practice. Islamic (swap-free) accounts are available on request.

## Regulation

City Index operates under StoneX Financial Ltd, which holds FCA authorisation (446717) — one of the world's strictest regulatory frameworks. This provides UK clients with mandatory FSCS protection up to £85,000 per person, negative balance protection, and segregated client funds.

Beyond the FCA, City Index holds ASIC (345646), CySEC (400/21), and MAS licences, covering Australia, the EU, and Singapore respectively. The parent company StoneX Group's NASDAQ listing adds an additional layer of financial transparency through SEC reporting requirements and quarterly earnings disclosures.

## Costs

City Index operates on a spread-only model for forex, indices, and commodities — zero commission with all costs embedded in the spread. EUR/USD starts from 0.5 pips (average around 0.9 pips), which is competitive for a market maker but wider than ECN brokers like IC Markets.

Share CFDs incur a separate commission of 0.08% per trade with a £10 minimum, which is reasonable for casual stock trading but adds up for active share traders. For UK traders using spread betting, profits are completely tax-free — a significant cost advantage over CFD alternatives where Capital Gains Tax applies.

The notable drawback is a £12/month inactivity fee after 12 months of no trading — easily avoided by placing one trade per year, but worth noting compared to competitors like Pepperstone and CMC Markets that charge nothing for inactive accounts.

## Deposits

City Index offers fee-free deposits via debit cards, credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. Card deposits are instant with a £100 minimum. Bank transfers have no minimum but take 1-3 business days. PayPal deposits are instant but require a £500 minimum.

Withdrawals are free for standard bank transfers (1-2 business days) and card returns (3-5 business days). Same-day CHAPS withdrawal is available for a £25 fee. The £25 CHAPS charge is a notable disadvantage — most competitors offer same-day withdrawal options for free.

The app's design is clean and intuitive, optimised for spread betting workflows. Push notifications keep traders informed of price movements and order fills. For mobile-first UK traders, the City Index app is genuinely among the best in the spread betting space.

## Support

City Index provides 24/5 customer support (Sunday 10am to Friday 5pm UK time) via phone, email, and live chat. Phone support is available on 0800 060 8609 (freephone) and 0203 194 1801. Live chat typically connects within 1 minute.

As a StoneX subsidiary, City Index benefits from institutional support infrastructure. However, email responses can take 2-3 business days for complex queries. Support is UK-based and knowledgeable about spread betting and tax-related questions specific to UK traders.

## Education

City Index offers a Trading Academy with structured courses covering spread betting basics, CFD trading, technical analysis, and risk management. Content is well-organised and suitable for beginners to intermediate traders, though not as comprehensive as IG Academy.

Research tools include Trading Central integration for technical analysis and automated trade ideas, SMS/email trading signals through a Faraday Research partnership (30-day free trial), and regular market commentary. The research offering is above industry average and well-integrated into the platform.
